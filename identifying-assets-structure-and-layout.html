Identifying assets / Structure and layout

Seb Jensen

To build upon and consolidate the pre-production efforts from my last two sessions, I am taking the time to identify the key assets or sorts of assets that I would need to use when reimagining the Rocket League Esports homepage in response to the brief. This will satisfy the short-term goal I set out in my aim to take a perspective approach to the progression of my project. Following this, I will also consider the structure and layout and user experience of the product I am adapting for a kiosk format and wider audience.

Identifying key assets

I have built upon the brief reference to the assets I would need by dedicating part of the pre-production process to identifying media and sorts of media that are relevant to the reworked product I am producing.

Structure and layout

Ensuring I focus on the structure and layout of my interactive esports product will benefit me greatly when I begin utilising media production skills and techniques to rework the Rocket League Esports homepage. It can serve as a checklist of sorts, a specification that I can follow to ensure that I am including all of the functionality, information, and interactive features to create and develop a good, seamless, and intuitive user experience.

Perspective project approach

Strengths include my direct response to my primary documented weakness from last session, the absence of substantial identified key assets. I have amended this by dedicating effort to the collation of various media that would be significant in my response to the brief.

Weaknesses include the limited sample of key assets I have identified. However, I believe I have a satisfactory selection of assets that would be significant in my response to the brief. It is important to note that the management of both time and effort is paramount in maintaining an effective and efficient workflow.

My short-term goal now concerns the production of wireframes and visualisation diagrams, and I intend to achieve this goal in the next session. This will conclude my pre-production process upon completion.

In terms of long-term goals, my focus will now begin to shift to the production process, with consideration for the post-production process that will follow it. I need to round up my pre-production work and efforts before beginning to collect and organise the assets I intend to use as well as creating new assets that I need.
