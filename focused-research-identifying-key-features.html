Focused research / Identifying key features

Seb Jensen

I believe the scope of the Rocket League Esports website could be easily reimagined in the format of a kiosk as to adapt it for a different audience. Consequently, it is necessary that I conduct further, focused research on said interactive esports product so that I can adequately rework its features and elaborate upon it professionally. In doing so, I would also identify the key features of the website, and I will then be able to reference back to this developed research and product specification during the production and post-production process.

I detailed a brief outline of what I would like to achieve (setting targets for myself). I also identified the key colour scheme, some media assets I would need to utilise, and listed the key features of the Rocket League Esports homepage. The product of this session's efforts are embedded below:

Perspective project approach

Strengths include my identification of key interactive features, such as the fan rewards gallery, that I would be able to translate into a kiosk format by using the trigger animation functionality included in PowerPoint. This will ensure a more effective and efficient design workflow as I will already have detailed the content I intend to include.

Weaknesses include the limited detailing of media that I would need to include when I reimagine the Rocket League Esports homepage. While I have mentioned the logo and two videos to include, there is little mention of what images I could include and modify to create my own assets.

My short-term goal is to identify the specific assets or sorts of assets that I would need to collect and collate to be used in my response to the brief. This rectifies the primary weakness of this session that I just detailed.

My long-term goal is to establish the structure and layout of the kiosk, supporting this with wireframe documents and, subsequently, the creation of various visualisation diagrams to support my production and post-production milestones.
