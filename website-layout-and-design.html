With the annotations I discuss the layout and the various choices I made. For example, consistently having the menu button and "Championship Series" badge in the same places in the header to ensure continuity. I also detail my use of colour and font to engage the user. I demonstrate how I have styled different elements to establish a visual hierarchy, too.

Navigation, as you can see, is all within a single slide. This means that I can make the buttons larger and easier to interact with for a kiosk-based audience. There are also hyperlinked buttons elsewhere in the product, linking to external resources and also taking the user to other slides. The extensive usage of various interactive features makes for an engaging experience.

Perspective project approach

I believe that I have had some success in reworking my chosen existing product, the Rocket League Esports website, and adapting it for a different audience. In this case, the switch is primarily from a website-based audience online to a kiosk-based audience. The main potential use case for my product would be in an exhibition hall at an esports-themed convention.

I think my user interface and experiences could have been even better if I more carefully considered the implementation of white space and usage of colour. While the bright, warm oranges and yellow/gold colours are engaging and captivating, the low contrast may impact the experience for users who can see less well or are less able to differentiate colours, for example. The layout is still quite cluttered, too, as you can see.

Now that I have combined my assets, my short-term goal pertains to exporting my interactive kiosk product. I intend to create a checklist of sorts that I can run through to identify any issues once I have exported it. After these checks, I will let my peers try my kiosk and leave feedback on the user experience.

My long-term goal is largely to start considering my final evaluation. I will review all of the comments I have left at the end of my blog entries as part of me taking a perspective approach to developing my project. I'll need to reference how the audience has changed and compare it back to the Rocket League Esports website.
